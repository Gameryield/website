GAMERYIELD(GYD) IS THE UTILITY AND YIELD TOKEN USED TO GOVERN AND INCENTIVIZE USERS IN THE ALL THE EARNING BASED DAPPS UNDER THE GAMERYIELDDAO UMBRELLA

TOKENOMICS

THE GAMERYIELD TOKEN TOKENOMIC MODEL MARRIES THE EXPLOSIVE GROWTH OF NFTS WITH THE SUPERIOR MONETIZATION CAPABILITIES OF DEFI. PARTICIPATE AND EARN REWARDS IN THE FORM OF ETHEREUM TOKENS (ETH), GAMERYIELD “GMRY” TOKENS AND NFT’S. AS A FUNDAMENTAL BUILDING BLOCK OF THE GAMERYIELD TOKEN MODEL IS BACKED BY A LIQUIDITY POOL WITH HIGH USAGE AND DEMAND VOLUME FROM MULTIPLE USE CASE DAPPS. EACH OF THE DAPPS ARE TIGHTLY INTEGRATED INTO DEX EXCHANGES SUCH UNISWAP AND USERS ARE HIGHLY INCENTIVIZED TO USE GAMERYIELD TOKEN INTERNALLY AND FOR EVERY CONTEXT WITHIN THE DAPPS. DURING DAPP USE, GAMERYIELD TOKENS ARE EITHER LOCKED UP OR BURNT, DEPENDING ON THE CONTEXT. NEW USERS OF DAPPS CAN DIRECTLY USE ETHER IN-DAPP WHICH IS IMMEDIATELY CONVERTED VIA UNISWAP TO GAMERYIELD TOKENS AND THEN BURNT, DECREASING SUPPLY AND INCREASING THE DEFLATIONARY EFFECT AND INCREASE IN GAMERYIELD TOKEN VALUATION AND PRICE.
